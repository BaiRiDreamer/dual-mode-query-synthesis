## Guidelines
1. Write from the user's perspective (what they want, not what was done)
2. Focus on the problem/need and desired outcome
3. The user may or may not:
   - Mention specific files or modules (depends on their technical level)
   - Propose implementation approaches (if they have ideas)
   - Use technical terminology (if they're technical)
   - Structure their request (some use markdown, some don't)
4. Capture the "why" behind the change
5. If it's a multi-step evolution, the user might outline phases/steps
6. Length: 100-400 words
7. Be natural - real users vary in clarity, detail, and style
